About Us


We are a group of incoming first year Waterloo Engineering students who wanted to make the application and transitioning process organized and more easily accessible to other students. We know how stressful the jump from high school to university can be, and how much research goes into making a decision. Waterloo Basics was designed to be an all-encompassing guide that has almost everything that is needed to become a Waterloo Warrior in one place. Additionally, we wanted to include our own experiences in order to highlight a student’s perspective to applying and transitioning. All of us come from different backgrounds and are going into different programs so we hope that our content reaches and helps as many people as possible!

Email us at waterloobasics@gmail.com if you have any questions. Feel free to use the discussions section of the website as well.
